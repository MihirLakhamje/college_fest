About

Utkarsh

Utkarsh, the prestigious college fest of S.K. Somaiya degree College of arts, science and commerce has earned an undisputed name in Mumbai college scene. it encompasses varied departments like Literary Arts, Performing Arts, Fine Arts, Gaming and sports. its a 3 day festivity wherein students, teachers and sponsors join hands to ensure those 3 days are not only entertaining butmemorable in every aspect. Utkarsh is a great platformfor students within the college and outside the college to showcase their talents and gain incomparable experience during their college life.

Somaiya

THE SOMAIYA VIDYAVIHAR CAMPUS COMPRISES OF 34 INDIVIDUAL INSTITUTESDEDICATEDTOARTS, SCIENCE, COMMERCE MANAGEMENT,HUMANITIES, PHILOSOPHIES AND SOCIAL SCIENCES AS IMPORTANT AREAS OF SCHOLARSHIP, AND IS SPREAD ACROSS A 64-ACRE (240,000 M2) COMPLEX IN VIDYAVIHAR, IN THE NORTH CENTRAL AREA OF MUMBAI AND A 90-ACRE (360,000 M2) COMPLEX INSION IN THE HEART OF MUMBAI. IT HAS OVER 35,000 STUDENTS AND 1,400 TEACHING STAFF. THE MAJORITY OF ITS COLLEGES ARE AFFILIATED TO THE UNIVERSITY OF MUMBAI. THE COLLEGE RUNS A FEW AUTONOMOUS POST-GRADUATE COURSES, VOCATIONAL TRAINING COURSES AND HIGH SCHOOLS WITHIN THE CAMPUS AS WELL AS IN RURAL INDIA SOMAIYA VIDYAVIHAR IS PRESIDED OVER BY JUSTICE P.N. BHAGWATI. FORMER CHIEF A JUSTICEOF INDIA. SHRI SAMIR SOMAIYA. A CORNELL UNIVERSITY AND HARVARD ALUMNUS IS THE PRESIDENT. BOTH PROVIDE INVALUABLE DIRECTION TO ALL THE EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTES WITHIN CAMPUS.
